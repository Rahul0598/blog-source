# Rahul Devajji

PhD Researcher · Urban Climate & Geospatial AI · Indiana University Bloomington

[research](/research/) · [cv](/resume.pdf) · [posts](/posts/) · [books](/readings/)

I'm Rahul - a PhD researcher in the [Healthy Cities Lab](https://healthycities.sice.indiana.edu) at Indiana University Bloomington, where I work with Dr. Dana Habeeb on urban climate and public health. I'm originally from Hyderabad and have lived in Vancouver and Bloomington.

Before the PhD I spent a few years in industry as a software developer, which is why a lot of my research ends up as working software: dashboards, sensor pipelines, and mapping tools that people outside academia actually use.

Outside the lab I read a lot of science fiction, follow spacecraft missions more closely than is probably healthy, and listen to too many podcasts. The [books](/readings/) page is where I keep track of what I've been reading.
