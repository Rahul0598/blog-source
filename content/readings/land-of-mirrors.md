I read this on a flight back from the Middle East. I felt like a child, fascinated by the book's wonderful artwork. At times, I sensed a deeper message that I was probably missing. I appreciated the depictions of loneliness, but I suppose I missed some of the deeper philosophical themes. When I got back, I did a quick Google search and found that others felt the same, or suggested the author intended the book to be open to multiple interpretations.
